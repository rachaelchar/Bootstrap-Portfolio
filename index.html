I'm currently enrolled in UNH's Coding Bootcamp, which is a full-stack web development program in Portsmouth, NH. I graduated from Allegheny College in 2017 with a BA in Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies and a minor in Classical Studies, then went on to work in marketing and events for a nonprofit animal shelter.

When I decided to make a change in my career, I chose to pursue coding because it encompasses the dichotomies of my ideal work environment: creativity and logic, and collaboration and self-reliance. The marriage of constant problem solving and being able to create and improve experiences and tools makes coding an engrossing subject for me.
